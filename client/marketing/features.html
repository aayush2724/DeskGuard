Features

Everything a busy
study space needs

From the live 3D map to the analytics dashboard your facilities team actually opens — here's everything included.

⬡

3D digital twin

Rotate, zoom, and click any desk for full status history. Embeds anywhere with a single script tag.

⚡

30-second refresh

Near-real-time occupancy without the cost or privacy concerns of video.

📊

Utilisation analytics

Heatmaps, peak-hour charts, and desk-level time-series exported to CSV or your BI tool.

🔒

Privacy-first

No video, no personal identifiers. GDPR compliant. Device signals are hashed on-device.

🗺️

Multi-floor mapping

Navigate between floors from a single view. Add new floors with a drag-and-drop floor plan import.

🔔

Booking integration

Pairs with LibCal, Outlook, and Google Calendar to show reserved vs walk-in desks at a glance.

👻

Ghost-seat detection

Distinguishes a bag left on a chair from an actual occupant, with a configurable timeout before it's flagged.

📱

Kiosk & mobile views

A lightweight kiosk mode for entrance displays, plus a responsive web view for students browsing on their phones.

🧩

Open API

Pull live occupancy into your own apps, signage, or campus map via a documented REST and WebSocket API.
