Ghost seat detection

Books don't count as people.

DeskGuard's ghost-seat classifier distinguishes between a person at a desk and a bag or laptop left to claim a seat. When a desk has been unoccupied for more than your policy window, it turns amber — and staff get a nudge.

  • ✓ Configurable ghost-seat timeout (15 min default)
  • ✓ Staff alert via Slack, Teams, or email
  • ✓ Full audit log for policy disputes
